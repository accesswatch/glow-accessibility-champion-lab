# Revised Concept Map Notes

## What Changed from Module 1

After completing all six modules, the concept map now reflects the full initiative:

- Human review is still at the center, but it is now informed by governance principles and fairness review.
- The GLOW workflow is now defined: Ground, Learn, Organize, Walk forward.
- Campus partners are the audience, not just a node.
- The AHG workshop is the public implementation milestone.

## Key Additions

- Fairness and bias review as a first-class step, not an afterthought.
- Accountability and disclosure as visible workflow components.
- The connection between AI capability, GLOW structure, and campus partner readiness.

## Revised Map Summary

The revised map shows how responsible AI capability, the GLOW framework, human review safeguards, and campus partner readiness combine to produce accessibility champions. The October AHG workshop tests and scales this model with a broader community.
