# Helpful, Risky, Human-Required Map

Use this map to categorize accessibility tasks before assigning them to an AI-supported workflow.

## Helpful: AI Can Assist with Low Risk

- Drafting accessible communication guidance using public examples
- Rewriting content for plain language and clarity
- Creating checklists for content owners
- Generating heading structure suggestions
- Drafting meaningful link text options
- Explaining WCAG criteria in plain language with verification

## Risky: AI Can Assist, but Requires Careful Human Review

- Generating alt text options (purpose requires human context)
- Reviewing documents for accessibility issues (automated checking is not sufficient)
- Drafting training examples (accuracy and tone must be verified)
- Suggesting remediation steps for complex documents

## Human Required: AI Should Not Make This Decision

- Final accessibility compliance determination
- Accommodation decisions or accommodation-specific guidance
- Legal interpretation of Section 508, ADA, or Title II
- Policy decisions affecting specific students or employees
- Disability-related HR decisions
- Medical or diagnostic information interpretation
