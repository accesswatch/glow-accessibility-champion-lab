# Forum Post Template

Use this template to draft your module discussion post.

## Opening: Connect to the Task

What did the module ask you to do, and what did you actually try?

## Capstone Connection

How does this module connect to your GLOW Accessibility Champion Lab capstone?

## One Artifact or Insight

Share one concrete thing you produced or learned. Keep it specific and accessible to peers who may not share your domain expertise.

## Peer Question

End with one genuine question for peers. Make it open-ended and inviting.

## Before Posting

- [ ] No sensitive, confidential, accommodation, or student information included.
- [ ] Accessibility claims are appropriately cautious.
- [ ] Tone is collegial and inviting.
- [ ] Post is 150–300 words.
- [ ] A copy is saved in the module folder.
