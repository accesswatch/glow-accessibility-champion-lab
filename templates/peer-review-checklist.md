# Peer Review Checklist

Use this checklist when reviewing a peer's AI-supported accessibility workflow or capstone artifact.

## Problem and Audience

- [ ] The accessibility problem is clearly defined.
- [ ] The audience is clearly described.
- [ ] The scope is realistic and bounded.

## Responsible AI Use

- [ ] Permitted and prohibited AI uses are defined.
- [ ] Human review is visible and specific.
- [ ] Privacy boundaries are clear.
- [ ] Disclosure approach is addressed.

## Accessibility and Equity

- [ ] Disability language is respectful and current.
- [ ] Multiple disability experiences are considered where relevant.
- [ ] Automated checking is not presented as sufficient.
- [ ] Accessibility expertise is included or referenced.

## Feasibility

- [ ] The workflow can be piloted within 90 days.
- [ ] The next steps are realistic and specific.
- [ ] Success indicators are practical.
