## Sanitization Checklist

### Remove or Replace

- [ ] Student names, IDs, or identifying information
- [ ] Employee names or HR-related details
- [ ] Accommodation records or disability-specific student information
- [ ] Medical, diagnostic, or health information
- [ ] Internal email addresses, phone numbers, or organizational details
- [ ] Confidential policy drafts or internal decision records
- [ ] Financial information or budget details
- [ ] Unpublished research or protected intellectual property

### Replace With

- Fictional names (e.g., "a student named Alex," "a faculty member," "a staff member in a large department")
- Generic roles and contexts
- Public or published examples
- Clearly labeled placeholder text

## After Sanitization

- [ ] Content reads naturally with fictional or generic details.
- [ ] No sensitive information remains.
- [ ] Content can be committed without harm to individuals or the institution.
