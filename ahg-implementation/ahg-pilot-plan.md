# AHG Pilot Plan

## Pilot Objective

Test the GLOW Accessibility Champion Lab workflow with a small group before the October 2026 AHG workshop.

## Timeline

- July 2026: Finalize one complete GLOW workflow with prompt, checklist, and teaching note.
- August 2026: Run internal pilot with two or three campus partners.
- September 2026: Revise based on pilot feedback. Prepare AHG workshop materials.
- October 2026: Deliver AHG workshop. Document outcomes. Share with participants.

## Pilot Participants

- Target: Two to three campus communicators or faculty members.
- Criteria: Regular creators of documents, announcements, or course materials.
- Commitment: One 60-minute session and brief follow-up survey.

## Pilot Activity

Participants will:

1. Receive a fictional or sanitized inaccessible document or announcement.
2. Follow a GLOW workflow using the structured prompt.
3. Review the AI output using the human review checklist.
4. Discuss what they learned and what felt uncertain.

## Feedback Collection

- Post-session reflection form.
- Follow-up email at two weeks: Did you change anything in your work?

## Pilot Success Criteria

- Participants understand the accessibility pattern.
- Participants can explain why human review is required.
- The facilitator identifies at least one improvement to the workflow.
