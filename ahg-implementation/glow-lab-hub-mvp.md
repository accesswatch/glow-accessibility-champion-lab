# GLOW Lab Hub MVP

## Purpose

The GLOW Lab Hub is the minimum viable product for sharing GLOW workflows, templates, and resources with campus partners and the broader accessibility community.

## Minimum Viable Features

1. A landing page explaining the GLOW framework.
2. At least one complete, human-reviewed GLOW workflow with prompt, checklist, and teaching note.
3. A human review checklist.
4. A privacy and safety guide.
5. A clear statement of what AI can and cannot do in accessibility work.

## Delivery Format

Primary format: GitHub wiki (wiki-source pages)

Secondary format: Sanitized public repository content

AHG format: Workshop handout and facilitation guide

## User Journeys

### Campus Communicator

Receives an inaccessible event announcement to improve. Opens GLOW workflow. Follows structured prompt. Reviews AI output. Applies checklist. Submits improved version.

### Faculty Member

Needs to make a course document more accessible. Uses GLOW alt text workflow. Understands why image purpose requires human judgment. Learns the accessibility pattern.

### Accessibility Professional

Uses GLOW Lab Hub to train and support campus partners. Delivers a 30-minute lab activity. Observes where partners struggle and refines the workflow.

## Success Indicators

- Partner can explain the accessibility pattern after the lab.
- Partner uses a checklist without being reminded.
- Partner identifies one task they will do differently next time.
