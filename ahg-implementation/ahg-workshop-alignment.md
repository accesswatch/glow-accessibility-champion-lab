# AHG Workshop Alignment

## Workshop Title

Accessibility Agents in Action: A Hands-On GLOW Workshop for Human-Centered Accessibility Workflows

## Subtitle

Helping Everyone Become an Accessibility Champion

## Conference

Accessing Higher Ground, October 2026

## Alignment with AI Campus Champions Capstone

The AHG workshop is the public validation and scaling milestone for the GLOW Accessibility Champion Lab developed through the AI Campus Champions program.

The capstone is not the AHG proposal. The capstone is a campus AI adoption initiative. The AHG workshop is how that initiative goes public and gets tested with a broader accessibility community.

## Workshop Promise

- Participants do not need to be AI scientists, developers, or programmers.
- Participants are met where they are.
- Participants work through practical accessibility problems.
- Participants design repeatable workflows.
- Participants learn to help partners become accessibility champions.

## Connection to Six Modules

| Module | What it contributes to AHG |
|---|---|
| 1 - AI Foundations | Shared vocabulary for participants |
| 2 - Generative AI | Prompt examples and hallucination awareness |
| 3 - AI Productivity | Workflow canvas participants use in the lab |
| 4 - Critical Thinking | Bias and fairness review built into lab activities |
| 5 - Governance | Acceptable use boundaries and privacy guardrails |
| 6 - Campus Champion | Lab design, facilitator guide, and initiative framing |
