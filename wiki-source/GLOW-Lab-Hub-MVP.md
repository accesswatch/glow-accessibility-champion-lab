# GLOW Lab Hub MVP

## Purpose

The GLOW Lab Hub is the minimum viable product for sharing GLOW workflows with campus partners and the broader accessibility community.

## Core Components

1. Landing explanation of the GLOW framework.
2. At least one complete, human-reviewed GLOW workflow with prompt, checklist, and teaching note.
3. Human review checklist.
4. Privacy and safety guide.
5. Clear statement of what AI can and cannot do in accessibility work.

## User Journeys

### Campus Communicator

Receives an inaccessible event announcement. Opens GLOW workflow. Follows structured prompt. Reviews AI output. Applies checklist. Submits improved version.

### Faculty Member

Needs to improve a course document. Uses GLOW alt text workflow. Understands why image purpose requires human judgment. Learns the accessibility pattern.

### Accessibility Professional

Uses GLOW Lab Hub to train campus partners. Delivers 30-minute lab activity. Observes where partners struggle and refines the workflow.

## Evaluation Approach

- Did participants understand the accessibility pattern?
- Did participants use the checklist without being prompted?
- Did participants identify one task they will do differently?
- Did participants know when to ask for expert help?
