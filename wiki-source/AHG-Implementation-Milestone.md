# AHG Implementation Milestone

## Conference

Accessing Higher Ground, October 2026

## Workshop Title

Accessibility Agents in Action: A Hands-On GLOW Workshop for Human-Centered Accessibility Workflows

## Role in the Capstone

The AHG workshop is the public validation and scaling milestone for the GLOW Accessibility Champion Lab. It tests the model with a broader accessibility community and creates an opportunity to refine workflows based on practitioner feedback.

## What the Workshop Does

- Introduces participants to the GLOW framework.
- Walks participants through one complete GLOW workflow activity.
- Demonstrates responsible AI use for accessibility coaching.
- Leaves participants with a reusable artifact and next step.

## Preparation Timeline

- July 2026: Finalize GLOW workflow and checklist.
- August 2026: Run internal pilot with campus partners.
- September 2026: Revise materials based on pilot feedback.
- October 2026: Deliver AHG workshop and document outcomes.

## What Success Looks Like

Participants leave with a completed GLOW workflow, a human-review checklist they can use immediately, and a clear understanding of what requires human judgment. The workshop produces at least one piece of community feedback that improves the model.
